Settlement Process

The life settlement process begins when a life insurance policyowner (life settlor) realizes that they no longer need the life insurance policy they took out years ago and ends with turning the unwanted life policy into cash.

How the Process Works

Policy Owners

If you decide to sell your life insurance policy to a life settlement provider, you will enter into a life settlement contract with them. The policy owner agrees to accept a cash payment in exchange for your life insurance policy.

Policy Owners Overview

Investors

Life settlements represent a new and exciting financial asset class and have attracted the attention of private and institutional investors. Life settlements are also considered an excellent way to diversify your investment portfolio.
